WHAT’S YOUR BURST STORY?

SQUEEZIT is a vitamin-enhanced tropical fruit juice made with lemon, mango, coconut, and orange. Real fruit juice provides added vitamins and delivers a refreshing burst of energy with every squeeze. Designed to be interactive and playful, the soft squeezable bottle creates a hands-on experience that reflects the brand’s energetic character. Alongside its vibrant attitude, SQUEEZIT maintains a mindful approach to sustainability by reducing excess materials and supporting easier recycling. SQUEEZIT balances energy, fun, and responsibility.

Playful by Nature, Mindful by Design

SQUEEZIT approaches sustainability through material reduction and thoughtful production. The bottle is made from squeezable plastic without an additional paper or plastic wrap label. Instead, product information and branding are printed directly onto the surface using washable ink that can be removed during the recycling cleaning process. By eliminating extra layers and adhesives, the design simplifies sorting and improves recyclability. This approach reduces material waste while maintaining the brand’s playful, functional form. SQUEEZIT prioritizes responsible packaging choices that support both user experience and environmental consideration.
