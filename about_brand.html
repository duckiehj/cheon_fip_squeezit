WHAT’S YOUR BURST STORY?

SQUEEZIT is a vitamin-enhanced tropical fruit juice made with lemon, mango, coconut, and o range. Real fruit juice provides added vitamins and delivers a refreshing burst of energy with every squeeze. Designed to be interactive and playful, the soft squeezable bottle creates a hands-on experience that reflects the brand’s energetic character. Alongside its vibrant attitude, SQUEEZIT maintains a mindful approach to sustainability by reducing excess materials and supporting easier recycling.
